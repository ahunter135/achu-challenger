Target heart rate zones are calculated using maximum heart rate - the highest number of times your heart can safely beat in one minute - withiny our age demographic

Peak:

Intensity: Vigorour (85 to 100 percent of your max heart rate)

Benefit: Increases performance speed

Cardio:

Intensity: Hard (70 to 84 percent of your max heart rate)

Benefit: Increases performance speed

Fat Burn:

Intensity: Moderate (50 to 69 percent of your max heart rate)

Benefit: Builds aerobic endurance and teaches the body to burn fat as fuel
